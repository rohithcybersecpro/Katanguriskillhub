About Katanguri Skill Hub

Learning should be simple, practical and enjoyable.

Katanguri Skill Hub was started with one vision — quality education in regional language.

Most students hesitate to ask questions because of language barriers. We conduct our sessions primarily in Telugu so every learner can confidently understand concepts and communicate freely.

We don't just teach technology. We prepare students for their careers by providing:

  • Career Roadmaps
  • Resume Preparation
  • Mock Interviews
  • Professional Communication
  • Workplace Etiquette
  • Common Sense & Professional Behaviour
